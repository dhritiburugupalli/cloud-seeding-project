## Study domain

Region: Colorado Rockies seeded target areas in/near the Central Colorado Mountain River Basin

Months: November–April

Years: 2020–2024

## Event definition (ISEF-level)

A “seeded storm event” is a continuous period where seeding operations are reported for a target area, mapped to a storm window (start–end date).

## Outcomes

Total precipitation over storm window (radar-derived estimate)

Snow fraction vs rain fraction (phase classification using temperature/elevation + radar/satellite indicators as you stated)
